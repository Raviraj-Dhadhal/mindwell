Privacy Policy

Your data is your own. Here’s how we protect it.

1. Data Collection

At MindWell, we only collect data that is strictly necessary for providing our AI insights and wellness tracking functions. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. All mood data and journal entries are securely encrypted string attributes.

2. Anonymous Tracking (Shadow Mode)

Users can opt into "Shadow Mode", which completely strips personally identifiable information (PII) from metadata before processing it with our AI backends. This ensures total anonymity when evaluating sensitive mental health issues.

3. Academic Success Integrations

If you opt to sync your academic calendars or timelines with the MindWell tool suite, the authorization tokens are stored encrypted and can be revoked at any time directly from the settings panel.

Last updated: August 2026. For privacy concerns, reach out via our contact page.
